Our Services

Groundwater Analysis

We provide advanced groundwater analysis services to assess water table levels near dumpyards and industrial sites. Using precise geospatial data and AI-driven predictions powered by the Gemini API, we deliver accurate insights into groundwater depth and potential contamination risks.

  • Latitude and longitude-based water table estimation with high precision
  • Refined predictions incorporating geological, hydrological, and environmental factors
  • Detailed reports outlining water table levels and potential leachate impacts

Environmental Impact Assessment

Our environmental impact assessments evaluate the effects of dumpyards on local ecosystems, soil integrity, and water resources. We analyze waste composition, rainfall infiltration, and groundwater interactions to provide comprehensive reports and actionable recommendations.

  • Analysis of plastic waste’s long-term effects on soil and water quality
  • Evaluation of rainfall-driven pollutant infiltration into groundwater
  • Recommendations for mitigating nitrogen pollution and hazardous chemical seepage

Waste Management Consulting

We offer expert consulting to optimize waste management practices, reducing environmental harm and enhancing public health safety. Our tailored strategies are based on your site’s specific data, ensuring practical and sustainable solutions.

  • Waste segregation plans for plastics, metals, glass, and biodegradables
  • Strategies to minimize nitrogen pollution from organic waste decomposition
  • Guidance on eco-friendly disposal, recycling, and landfill management

Custom Reporting & Email Delivery

Receive detailed, professional PDF reports delivered straight to your inbox. Our reports combine raw data, AI-generated insights, and expert recommendations, customized to your location and project requirements.

  • Downloadable PDF reports with clear visualizations and analysis
  • Secure, instant email delivery for accessibility
  • Option to request periodic updates or follow-up assessments
